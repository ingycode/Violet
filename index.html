Parents:

Violet is the eldest of four children. Her mother was a former adventuress who left to join an army at war when Violet was eight.

“Daughter, we must find our duties in what comes to us, not in what we imagine might have been... or something.”

-Lenora Kimberley

Her father was a not-all-that-successful musician. He was a kind father, but did not provide all that much.

“Violet, expect the best, prepare for the worst, and capitalise on what comes. Remember that, my girl.”

-Bennett Bonnington

Violet learned to make her own money early on. As a child she was always in trouble with teachers and other adults, but had a talent for talking her way out.
